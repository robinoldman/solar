ECO4 – Elevating Home Energy Efficiency

Zenith Eco Solutions is proud to be a participant in the fourth phase of the ECO scheme. This ongoing 4-year initiative is dedicated to elevating the energy efficiency of homes, reducing energy costs, and promoting a greener environment.

Affordable Warmth Obligation – Your Gateway to Energy Efficiency

You could be eligible for energy-saving home improvements if you are a private housing resident claiming specific benefits or reside in social housing.

Eligibility Criteria for Private Housing Residents:

  • Child Tax Credit
  • Housing Benefit
  • Working Tax Credit
  • Universal Credit
  • Pension Credit
  • Income Support
  • Income-based Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA)
  • Income-related Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Child Benefit

Note: Renters must obtain the property owner's consent to proceed with the work.

Potential Benefits Include:

  • Insulation enhancements for lofts or cavity walls
  • Boiler replacements or repairs
  • Upgrades to efficient heating solutions like air source heat pumps and other renewable sources
